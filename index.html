Questions we always get.

Do I need to prepare scripts or talking points?
+
No scripts needed — and we actually prefer it that way. Scripted content reads as scripted on camera. We prep everything before the day and interview you conversationally, which is what makes short-form video feel authentic and perform well. You just need to know your subject — and you already do.
Where does the shoot happen?
+
We come to you. Your office, clinic, studio, home — wherever you're most comfortable and wherever makes sense for your brand. We'll advise on the best setup during your prep call. If you don't have a good space, we can discuss studio options as well.
I'm not comfortable on camera. Is that a problem?
+
It's actually the most common thing we hear — and it's never been a dealbreaker. Our interview approach is specifically designed to make people who don't like being filmed feel comfortable. The warmup session helps a lot, and when you're talking about something you know deeply, it shows. Most clients tell us they forgot we were filming within the first 20 minutes.
How long does the shoot day actually take?
+
Plan for a half-day — typically 3 to 4 hours including setup and interview sessions. That single session is what we draw all your content from across the full 3 months — batched and posted monthly. A well-prepped half-day generates more usable footage than you'd expect, and the focused format keeps your energy high throughout.
How does the monthly posting work?
+
After your shoot, we edit and batch your content and release it on a monthly schedule — posting directly to your Instagram, TikTok, and/or YouTube Shorts on your behalf. You approve the content before anything goes live. The cadence (daily, a few times a week, etc.) is built around your audience and goals. You keep full access to your accounts at all times.
Will this actually grow my following?
+
Short-form video is the highest-ROI content format on social right now — especially for experts and business owners. Three months of consistent, professional content gives the algorithm something to work with and gives your audience a reason to follow. We can't guarantee specific follower numbers, but we can guarantee that by month three you'll have real data, real engagement, and a clear picture of what continued investment will do. That's exactly why we structured this as a 3-month starter — it's enough time to actually see what's possible.
What happens after the 3 months?
+
No pressure and no automatic renewal. At the end of month three, we'll walk you through your results — growth, engagement, top-performing content — and talk through what continued partnership looks like. Clients who continue move to our full ongoing content program at $3,500/month, which includes a fresh shoot every quarter, fresh content delivered and posted monthly, and ongoing performance reporting. Most clients find the results make that decision easy — but you're never obligated to continue.
